SmartPik Plugin - Images with one-click sharing, zoomability, and embed anywhere.

== Description ==

[SmartPik](http://smartpik.com/plugin) is image sharing made incredibly easy with strong social features and plenty of va va zoom. SmartPik is for website and blog publishers who want to promote their images and encourage their audience to share their content.

Images are enhanced with the SmartPik toolbar and real smarts are added like one-click sharing, zoomability, and embed anywhere. Don't forget to check out the [example screenshot](http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/smartpik/screenshots/) and the [FAQ](http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/smartpik/faq/).

We would love to hear your thoughts and experience with SmartPik. [Get in touch with us](http://smartpik.com/contact) or stay in touch by following us on [Twitter](http://twitter.com/smartpik) or [Facebook](http://www.facebook.com/pages/smartpik/142390105824180) :)

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

1. Upload `smartpik` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What does SmartPik do with blog images? =

SmartPik adds a toolbar to the bottom of images that lets users do one-click sharing, zoom the image to full-screen, and embed the image anywhere.

= Does SmartPik upload, store or modify images? =

No. SmartPik does NOT upload, store or strip copyright metadata from images.

= Will SmartPik work on a local WordPress installation? =

No. We only support websites and images that are readily accessible.

= What images are supported? =

Any image that its width is no smaller than 190 pixels.
